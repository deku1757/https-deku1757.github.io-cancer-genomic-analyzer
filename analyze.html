💡 What is VCF?

VCF (Variant Call Format) is a standard text format for representing DNA sequence variations. It contains information about genetic mutations and variants found in your genome.

❓ Need Help?

How to get your VCF file:

  • DNA testing services (23andMe, AncestryDNA, etc.) can provide raw DNA data
  • Medical sequencing labs provide VCF files with genetic test results
  • GATK or SAMtools can generate VCF files from BAM files
  • Most genomic databases allow VCF file download

What genes are analyzed:

  • BRCA1 - Breast and ovarian cancer risk
  • BRCA2 - Breast and pancreatic cancer risk
  • TP53 - Li-Fraumeni syndrome, multiple cancers
  • PTEN - Cowden syndrome, breast cancer
  • MLH1 - Lynch syndrome, colorectal cancer
