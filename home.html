Key Genes We Analyze

BRCA1

Risk Level: 92%

Tumor suppressor gene. Mutations significantly increase breast and ovarian cancer risk.

BRCA2

Risk Level: 87%

Similar to BRCA1, mutations increase breast, ovarian, and pancreatic cancer risk.

TP53

Risk Level: 89%

Guardian of the genome. Mutations can lead to multiple cancer types.

PTEN

Risk Level: 85%

Tumor suppressor gene associated with breast and other cancers.
